Quiet Zone

📍 0.3 mi away
Location
Yerba Buena Gardens — Howard St between 3rd & 4th, San Francisco, CA 94103
Hours
Open sunrise to 10:00 PM daily
What to Expect
Shaded benches along the east side. Low foot traffic on weekdays after 2 PM. Grass area to sit or lie down. Water fountain near the playground entrance.
Tips
The terrace behind the waterfall is the quietest spot. Good place for a 5-minute breather between deliveries.
