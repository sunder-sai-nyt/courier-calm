Theft Awareness Tips

Before Each Stop
Lock your vehicle every single time, even for 30-second drops. Take your phone and keys with you. Keep delivery bags closed and out of sight.
While Delivering
Park in well-lit, visible areas. Avoid leaving packages visible in your vehicle. Be aware of your surroundings when entering unfamiliar buildings.
If Something Happens
Do not resist or confront. Move to a safe location. Call 911 for emergencies or 311 to file a report. Document everything: time, location, description.
Useful Contacts
Emergency: 911
Non-emergency report: 311
